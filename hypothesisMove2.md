# MOVE 2: THE BET

## Claim

I believe that most learners who can correctly define frontend and backend will struggle to explain why a backend must exist when challenged with a first-principles architectural question.

## Result that proves me right

70% or more users can define frontend and backend but fail to provide a first-principles explanation for why backend systems are necessary.

## Result that means I built a mirror

Users only repeat definitions they already know, and the tool fails to uncover any deeper misunderstanding.

## Kill-number

If fewer than 20% of users demonstrate this gap, I will conclude that my hypothesis is wrong and that the problem is not common enough to justify the tool.
